# Viewing Total Earnings

## Overview

Track and manage your financial success directly from the user-friendly dashboard. The AI Image Generator provides a transparent and detailed view of your total earnings, empowering you to gauge the impact of your creative endeavors.

## Accessing Total Earnings

Navigate to the "Earnings" section on your dashboard to access a comprehensive breakdown of your income from the AI-generated stock images. Here's how you can interpret and utilize this information:

### 1. Total Earnings Overview

The dashboard displays a summary of your total earnings. This includes the cumulative revenue generated from all your uploaded images.

### 2. Earnings Breakdown

Dive deeper into your earnings with a detailed breakdown. Explore data such as:

   - **Image Views:** Track how many users have viewed your images on Adobe Stock.
   - **Downloads:** Monitor the number of times your images have been downloaded by customers.
   - **Revenue:** See the financial returns from your stock photography portfolio.

### 3. Time-Based Analytics

Utilize the time-based analytics feature to understand the performance trends of your stock images over different periods. Whether it's daily, weekly, or monthly, this tool empowers you to identify patterns and make informed decisions.

## Leveraging Insights

Understanding your total earnings and the associated analytics enables you to:

- **Optimize Content:** Identify popular images and create similar content to maximize your earnings.
- **Refine Strategy:** Use data to refine your content strategy, focusing on what resonates most with your audience.
- **Enhance Earnings:** Make informed decisions on how to increase your overall revenue based on the performance of your stock images.
