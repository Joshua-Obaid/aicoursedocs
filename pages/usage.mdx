# Using the AI Image Generator

## Overview

The AI Image Generator is designed to make the process of creating and monetizing stock images a seamless experience. Whether you're a seasoned photographer or a budding artist, this section will guide you through the simple steps to harness the power of AI for image generation and passive income.

## Getting Started

### 1. Subscription

Choose a subscription package that aligns with your needs. Select from the Basic, Standard, or Premium packages, each offering a specific number of credits to use for image uploads.
